About

Our primary objective is to provide people of all ages and background a platform to gain financial literacy and freedom. We live in a society where millions of people are either living in poverty or with crippling debt. They work effortlessly to save money for a better future but keep making common mistakes and delaying the process.

That's where our webiste comes into play. We provide information, tools, practice simulations and guidance to avoid making some of the common yet easily avoidable mistakes. Its very simple to use and can be used to store past data, results and goals.

Our featured section is predicting stock prices using machine learning. Our accuracy is within 10% of the actual price (on a very small sample set). With more information we plan to improve the accuracy. We also plan to include sections for predicting the price using mathematical models. They will soon be added.

DISCLAIMER: In no shape of form Finance Plus is liable for losses. The information is not a professional information. Please use it at your own risk.

The next steps is to develop a designated section for kids with simple interactive games to teach them about money management from a early stage of life. This feature could be used in highschools and by parents for educational purposes free of cost.
